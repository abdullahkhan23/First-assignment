Almarwad Intl. Associates in Projects

Almarwad Intl. has partnered with major companies in tank building EPC contracting, MEI EPC contracting, MEP & building automation EPC Contracting to cater the requirements of Kuwait Industrial sector. Our major partners are listed below, where Almarwad Intl. works just not as agent, but as a local partner working with all the industrial sectors.

Vishal Structurals Pvt. Ltd.

Vishal has successfully completed more than 750 tanks in the Oil & Gas sector in India and Africa up to the size of 65 M diameter.

Vishal's experience in designing and constructing tankages is an impressive panorama of engineering skills, organisational capabilities and a challenging spirit.

Floating roof, Cone roof and Cryognic dome roof tanks, Vishal has designed and constructed with all possible variations of types and sizes. Vishal has completed most of the projects on schedule, inspite of deterrants, constraints and difficult situations.

Shriram EPC.

Shriram EPC is one of India's leading companies in this highly specialised field, bringing much more to the table than the ability to execute large and complex projects on a turnkey basis. SEPC provide customised solutions for projects, from concept to completion, with a strong in-house design, engineering and project management team.
A US$240million company, Shriram EPC is the engineering arm of the Shriram Group and is engaged in turnkey contracting, developing projects that focus on renewable energy, design and implementation of projects for water & sewage treatment plants. Over a period of time, SEPC have acquired the skills to collaborate and work in tandem with world leaders in specific areas as part of multi-country consortia.
